How to Apply for Voter ID Card

Why Your Voter ID Matters

Voter ID is a key identity document in India, giving you the right to vote and serving as proof of citizenship. It also acts as an official ID for various purposes.

Who Can Apply?

Any Indian citizen aged 18 years or above is eligible to apply for a Voter ID. You must also be a resident of the constituency where you are applying.

Steps to Apply Online

  1. Visit NVSP Portal
  2. Click Sign Up and create an account using mobile/email
  3. Login and select Form 6
  4. Fill all required details and upload documents
  5. Preview and submit your application

Steps to Apply Offline

  1. Visit the nearest State Election Office
  2. Collect and fill Form 6
  3. Attach required documents
  4. Submit the form at the office

Semi-Online Method

  1. Download Form 6 from NVSP Portal
  2. Fill the form and attach documents
  3. Submit the form to the nearest election office

Documents Required

  • ID Proof (Aadhaar, Driving License, Passport)
  • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Bank Passbook)
  • Date of Birth Proof (Birth Certificate, Passport)
  • Passport-size photograph

Check Voter ID Status

  1. Visit NVSP Portal
  2. Login to your account
  3. Click Track Application Status
  4. Enter your reference number and state

Things to Remember

  • Applicant must be at least 18 years old
  • Ensure documents match the details in Form 6
  • After receiving Voter ID, verify all details
  • Apply for correction immediately if there are errors
