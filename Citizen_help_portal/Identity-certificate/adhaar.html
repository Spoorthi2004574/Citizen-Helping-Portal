How to Apply for Aadhaar Card

Who can apply?

Any Indian resident or individual who has lived in India for 182 days or more in the last 12 months can apply for Aadhaar. Even children below 5 years can apply for Baal Aadhaar.

Steps to Apply Online

  1. Visit UIDAI Website
  2. Click on My Aadhaar → Book an Appointment
  3. Select your city and proceed to book an appointment
  4. Visit Aadhaar Seva Kendra with ID, Address, and DOB proofs
  5. Provide biometrics and submit documents
  6. Get an acknowledgment slip for tracking
  7. Aadhaar card delivered within 90 days

Steps to Apply Offline

  1. Visit the nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra
  2. Fill and submit the enrollment form
  3. Provide biometrics and documents
  4. Get acknowledgment slip for tracking

Important Documents

Proof of Identity

  • Passport
  • PAN Card
  • Voter ID
  • Driving License

Proof of Address

  • Bank Statement
  • Electricity Bill
  • Ration Card

Proof of DOB

  • Birth Certificate
  • PAN Card

Check Aadhaar Status

  1. Visit UIDAI Website
  2. Click on Check Aadhaar Status
  3. Enter Enrollment ID and Captcha
  4. View status online
