How to Apply for Passport in India

Why Passport is Important?

A passport is a vital document for international travel and serves as proof of identity and citizenship. It is issued by the Ministry of External Affairs through Passport Seva Kendras (PSKs).

Eligibility Criteria

  • Indian nationals aged 18 years or above
  • Minors below 18 years can apply with parental consent
  • Minors aged 15-18 can apply for 10-year validity passports

Steps to Apply Online

  1. Visit Passport Seva Portal
  2. Register and create an account
  3. Select “Apply for Fresh Passport / Re-issue”
  4. Fill and submit the application form
  5. Schedule an appointment and make payment
  6. Visit PSK with documents on the appointment date

Steps to Apply Offline

  1. Get the application form from the nearest Passport Office or DPC
  2. Fill the form and attach required documents
  3. Pay the fees via Demand Draft
  4. Submit the form at the Passport Office and get an acknowledgement slip

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Voter ID / PAN Card
  • Birth Certificate (for minors)
  • Utility Bill / Rent Agreement (as address proof)
  • Passport-size photographs

Passport Fees

  • 36 pages (10-year validity): ₹1,500 (Tatkal: ₹2,000)
  • 60 pages (10-year validity): ₹2,000 (Tatkal: ₹2,000)
  • Minor (below 18): ₹1,000
  • Lost/Damaged Passport: ₹3,000 - ₹3,500

Track Passport Status

  1. Visit Passport Seva Portal
  2. Click on “Track Application Status”
  3. Enter your file number and date of birth

Processing Time

Normal passport: 30-45 days

Tatkal passport: 7-14 days
