How to Apply for International Driving Permit in India

What is an International Driving Permit?

An International Driving Permit (IDP) is a document that allows you to drive a motor vehicle in foreign countries that are signatories to the 1949 Geneva Convention on Road Traffic. It translates your driving license into multiple languages and is recognized in over 150 countries.

Uses of an IDP

  • Allows you to legally drive in foreign countries
  • Serves as an identity proof abroad
  • Helps in renting vehicles internationally
  • Accepted in case of traffic checks overseas

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must hold a valid Indian Driving License
  • Minimum age: 18 years
  • Application must be made within 1 year of issue of your domestic license

Documents Required

  • Valid Indian Driving License
  • Copy of Passport
  • Copy of Visa
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Medical Certificate (Form 1A)
  • Proof of Address
  • Application Form 4A for IDP

How to Apply

  1. Visit the Parivahan Portal or your nearest RTO
  2. Fill out Form 4A and upload required documents
  3. Pay the applicable IDP fee
  4. Submit the form online or at the RTO
  5. Collect your IDP after processing (usually 3–5 days)

Validity & Renewal

  • Valid for 1 year from date of issue
  • Cannot be renewed directly — must apply for a new IDP
  • Requires valid domestic license at the time of application
