How to Apply for a Senior Citizen Card in India

What is a Senior Citizen Card?

A Senior Citizen Card is an identity document for Indian citizens aged 60 and above. It helps access special benefits, discounts, and services reserved for senior citizens. You can apply via your state’s official website or nearest Sewa Kendra by filling a form and paying a nominal fee of ₹10.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Indian citizen aged 60 years or above
  • Valid proof of age
  • Proof of permanent residency in the respective state

Benefits of Senior Citizen Card

  • Higher interest rates on deposits
  • Income tax exemptions
  • Medical discounts at private and government hospitals
  • Priority hearing at High Courts
  • Priority BSNL/MTNL connection installation
  • Access to old age services at low charges
  • Discounts on state-government bus services

Documents Required

Identity Proof:
  • Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, Driving License, Pension Card, Govt-issued ID, Bank Certificate
Address Proof:
  • Passport, Aadhaar, Voter ID, Ration Card, Rental Agreement, Registered Sale Deed, Revenue Record, Telephone Bill, Bank Passbook, Voter List
Age Proof:
  • Passport, Birth Certificate, PAN Card, School Pass-out Certificate(Optional)

How to Download the Application Form

  1. Go to the official National Portal of India.
  2. Navigate to ‘Senior Citizen Identity Card’ page.
  3. Select ‘Application Form for Issue of Identity Card to Senior Citizen’.
  4. Click the link to open the form page.
  5. Download and print the form.

How to Apply Online

  1. Visit the official National Government Services portal.
  2. Open your state’s senior citizen card application page.
  3. Fill in the form and upload documents and photo.
  4. Pay the registration fee.
  5. Submit the form online.

How to Apply Offline

  1. Visit the General Tehsildar’s office or local government office.
  2. Collect and fill the application form.
  3. Attach necessary documents.
  4. Pay the applicable fees.
